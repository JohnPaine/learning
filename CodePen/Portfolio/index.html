About Myself


Hello everyone! I'm a desktop programmer with 4+ years exp.
For now, I work at Holding Informtest in Moscow as a c++ programmer. I write and maintain c++ programs with Qt framework for Windows that use our self-produced hardware to build amazing solutions for complex measurement and control systems for civil and military usage.
I love programming at all and my current job in particular. But I always wanted to study web programming because it offers a vast amount of unexplored opportunities for me as I think.
I fulfill tasks from FreeCodeCamp web school to claim my Front-end developer certificate for now. It's a great course that offers cool modern stuff to study totally for free! The only things that needed are your time and interest. I advise this course to everyone even if you don't see yourself as a web-programmer, as it's greatly broaden programming scope for those who take it seriously and can offer many job opportunities in future. Good luck to everyone and thanks for your time!
