Holding Informtest


Holding "Informtest" was formed in 1996 by group of companies engaged in engineering of control and diagnostic systems for ground electronic equipment and space vehicles control equipment, data transfer systems and space communication systems.
The oldest company of holding was engaged in terminal data processing systems engineering in 1990.
Specialists of holding's companys have more than 20 years experience of various purpose data processing and transfer systems development and exploitation.
At the end of 2003 our stuff is 96 employees including 1 doctor and 1 candidate of technical science. Average age of the employees is 34 years.
Own range of VXI-instruments developed by Informtest constantly grows at average on 10% per year.
